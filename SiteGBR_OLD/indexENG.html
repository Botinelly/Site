Welcome to GBR Componentes

The GBR Componentes is the newest company of a traditional empresarial group of Amazonas, the GRUPO GARCIA.

Operating in the Manaus market for 76 years, GRUPO GARCIA has diversified to meet very different market demands in the areas of telecommunications and television signal retransmission, construction, real estate investments and representation of GM and Citröen brands, among other activities.

Counting with a modern industrial park, last generation equipments and trained professionals, the GBRComponentes provides tecnological services such as audio and video producs, computing, telecommunication among others.

Politics

        

The Management of GBRComponentes da Amazônia LTDA , in accordance with the requirements of ISO 9001: 2015, commits itself to maintaining the continuous improvement of its QMS by attending:

        

a) Ensure excellence in customer service through strict quality control in our processes, products and services.

        

b) Meet the requirements of our legal and other associates.

        

c) Manage the risks and opportunities that may impact the organization.

        

d) Promote the engagement of all employees, contributing to an excellent result.

      
      

Mission

        

Acting in the market in an ethical, safe, profitable, legal way, with social and environmental responsibility, providing products and services, adapting the needs of our customers and contributing to the development of the region.

      
      

Vision

        GBR seeks to reach new markets, being a benchmark in its segment of operations, in the provision of services, respecting legal requirements, seeking new technologies, constant training of our employees and respecting the environment

      
      

Values​​

        

• Ethics

• Transparency

            Share information about the organization with our internal and external collaborators.         

• Excellence in Products and Services - Focus on Outcome

            Offer solutions with high added value to our customers, meeting their needs and expectations.         

• Enhancing Intellectual Capital

            Contribute to the continued development of our employees, as well as to value their skills and contributions to achieve organizational results.         

• Commitment to Results - Focus on Outcome

            Constantly seek the achievement of the proposed goals to achieve the desired results.         

• Social, Economic and Environmental Sustainability
